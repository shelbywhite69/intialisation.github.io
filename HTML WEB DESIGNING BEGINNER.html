Expectations vs Reality

Expectations:

  • A huge , tremendous and permanent campus.
    • A sublime canteen/cafeteria.
    • Lots of trees.
    • Electric bike(bicycle) rental stand.
    • Roads to ride electric bikes/bicycles.
  • Freedom to roam anywhere , anytime.
  • Lots of co-curricular activities.
    • Gym.
    • Lawn Tennis.
    • Bands.
    • Squash.
    • Swimming Pool
    • Atleast 4 tables for ping pong.
  • Vivacious Fests.
  • A grand fresher's party.
  • Privacy.
  • Smooth life (faster internet, no power cuts, lots of free time to explore and introspect)
  • Less academic pressure.
  • Good food(atleast digestable if not scrumptious).

Reality:

  • Only a few co-curricular activities are available.
  • Short semester, thus, a lot of academic pressure.
  • No privacy and too many restrictions.
  • Rented campus and facilities.
  • Uninviting & unethical mess.
  • Very few fests.
